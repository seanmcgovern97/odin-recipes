Spaghetti Bolognese

In this recipe you will learn how to make Spaghetti Bolognese. This is a dirty oul Spaghetti Bolognese that tastes like mother teresa's toe nails.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef or minced meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Canned tomatoes

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti pasta according to package directions until it is al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat some olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add the ground beef or minced meat to the skillet and cook until it is browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Add the chopped onion and garlic to the skillet and continue cooking until the onion is translucent and fragrant.
  5. Pour in the canned tomatoes and stir everything together well.
  6. Bring the mixture to a simmer and then reduce the heat to low.
  7. Let the bolognese sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  8. Serve the bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti pasta, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
