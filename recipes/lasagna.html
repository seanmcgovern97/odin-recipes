Lasagna

In this recipe you will learn how to make Lasagna. This is a dirty oul lasagna that smells like gandis flip flops.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (either oven-ready or traditional)
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • Water or broth
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian seasoning or herbs like basil, oregano, and thyme
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Spinach or other veggies (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Add the onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened. Stir in the tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water or broth, salt and pepper, and Italian seasoning or herbs. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, 1 cup of the shredded mozzarella cheese, 1/2 cup of the grated Parmesan cheese, and the egg.
  5. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Spoon half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles and spread it evenly. Repeat the layers of meat sauce, noodles, and ricotta cheese until all the ingredients are used up.
  6. Top with the remaining shredded mozzarella cheese and grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the lasagna is hot and cooked through.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
